Distant Dreams

Scheduled for release December 2015, Distant Dreams and Love Songs is a nine movement chamber orchestral suite I wrote for piano, strings, flute, bassoon, and percussion. The recording with live chamber orchestra, crowd-funded through Kickstarter, will take place this fall.

Elise

Elise (2015) is a twelve-minute documentary that follows a young, transgender, and homeless woman who, because of multi-layered discrimination and economic oppression, has been forced to engage in survival sex along the streets of Washington, D.C. I composed the original score.

Music for Film

My scores combine orchestral, jazz, and electronic influences. I am as influenced by the likes of Howard Shore, Michael Giacchino, Hans Zimmer, and Joe Hisaishi as I am by Ravel, Debussy, Bill Evans, and Keith Jarrett.

Composition

I score for various forms of film, online and televised advertisement, documentary and radio. I specialize in composing original scores, though I also have a large library of music available for licensing.

Production

I work with artists in genres including rock, soul, hip-hop, folk, a capella, and electronic. I help the artists I work with realize the potential of their recorded music. I can also offer my skills as an instrumentalist to projects.

Piano

I perform as a pianist. I also teach piano to students of all ages and experience. My approach balances developing the fundamentals necessary to engage with the instrument with explorations into improvisation and theory.

Bio

Music has been my driving force for longer than I can remember. It has been variously an inspiration, a companion through sadness, a fellow partier, an intellectual pursuit, and an exciting line of work.

I began playing piano around the time I started elementary school, and I picked up other instruments and melodies across my childhood and adolescence, twisting variously into pursuits through classical, rock, jazz, and electronic musics. Towards the end of my time in high school, I began composing - both for small commercial jobs where I could get them and creative projects where I found them - and I've worked in college and afterward to push my creativity, musicianship, and composition skills to the utmost. Alongside I've developed passions for recording, mixing, and teaching and I work professionally in all these areas.

My goals are to create moving soundtracks for beautiful films, work with other inspired people to help them achieve the sounds they imagine, and never stop learning more about the art of making good music.
