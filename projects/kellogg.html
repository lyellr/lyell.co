W. K. Kellogg Foundation Oral Health Campaign

The W. K. Kellogg Foundation commissioned a conference and series of television spots to raise awareness about the oral health care access crisis affecting some parts of the United States, particularly New Mexico.

This video was accompanied by a series of shorter spots, each of which needed a distinct theme based around setting. To this end, as the shots move through various parts of the country, the music reflects the changing scenes and stories.
