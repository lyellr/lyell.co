Silver Alert

Gino Scofidio's Silver Alert was a submission to the 2014 48 Hour Film Project, Washington D.C. I composed the original score during the second day of the competition. The requirements for this 48 hour competition were:

  • Genre: Western
  • Character: Samuel or Samantha Canterbury, an event planner
  • Line: "You can trust me."
  • Prop: an envelope
