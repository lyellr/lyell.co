Film/Game Scores


I compose dynamic, compelling music for film, video game, and advertisement. I also provide in house SFX, foley, mixing, mastering, and voiceover.

I began playing piano as a child, variously studying jazz and classical performance and composition. My scores draw from my roots playing and studying jazz and classical piano, my experience writing and producing modern electronic music, and my studies of narrative forms as a writer and composer.

Production

Embers was the third single from Tigers Are Bad For Horses. I produceed, co-wrote the track, and played the majority of the instrumental. Embers to date has over 300,000 streams online.


Distant Dreams and Love Songs

Listen Here
Buy on iTunes

Distant Dreams and Love Songs is a nine movement chamber orchestral suite I wrote in early 2015 and recorded in January of 2016. Arranged for piano, strings, flute, bassoon, and percussion, DDLS explores the hope, fear, and isolation that distance imposes on love.

Solo Piano


I perform in Los Angeles and around the country as a solo pianist or with a trio or combo. My pianistic style is informed by training as a jazz pianist as well as explorations into classical and contemporary music as a pianist and composer.

My style and repertoire provide an emotive, refined performance that has enhanced the atmosphere of clubs, restaurants, hotels, and other venues looking to push their live music beyond the norm. For booking and rates please email booking@lyell.co.

Music Instruction


I teach piano and music production/composition private lessons in the Los Angeles area and remotely over Skype to adults and children of all ages. I tailor lessons to your schedule and learning goals, balancing fundamentals with improvisation techniques, repertoire development, and a relaxed learning environment. Please contact me for more information or to chat about your learning goals.
